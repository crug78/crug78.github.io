the different components

guitars are made up of a few different major components, such as:

  • the body
  • the bridge
  • the fretboard
  • the neck
  • the headstock
  • the pickups
  • the strings
  • the volume and tone knobs

    • the body, fretboard, and neck are the base components of the guitar. the strings are threaded around the back through the bridge, which holds the strings in place. the strings go across the fretboard and into the tuning pegs in the headstock. the tuners are used to hold the strings at the top, and to tune them. the pickups are used to pickup the vibrations of the strings using magnets. it's similar to several very small microphones for each string. most guitars have 2 pickups, one of the bridge and one for the neck.
