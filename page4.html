there are many different ways that you can tune a guitar. here are some of the most common guitar tunings.
tuning name description guitar type tuning
E Standard the most commonly using tuning 6 string EADGBE
Drop D E Standard, but with the low E tuned to a D 6 string DADGBE
D Standard One half step lower from E Standard 6 string DGCFAD
Drop C One half step lower from Drop D 6 string CGCFAD
