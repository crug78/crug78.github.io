extended range guitars

most guitars only have 6 strings, but some, called extended range guitars, have more. the first 7 string guitar was created by ibanez for steve vai. they were then used by the band korn in their music, creating a new movement of lower tuned guitars in metal.

some other famous guitarists who use extended range guitars are:

  • fredrick thorndall (meshuggah) - first person to use an 8 string
  • justin lowe (after the burial) - 8 string
  • lucas mann (rings of saturn) - 9 string
  • mick gordon (doom eternal) - 8 string
